About Agents

Agents are addresses that have special permissions on the blockchain:

  • Identity Registry Agent: Can add/remove investors from the token's identity registry
  • Token Agent: Can mint/burn tokens and perform other token operations
  • Compliance Agent: Can modify compliance rules (not yet implemented)
Note: Adding/removing agents requires blockchain interaction and may incur gas fees.

Agent Roles & Permissions
Identity Registry Agents
  • Register new identities
  • Update identity information
  • Add/remove claims
  • Verify KYC status
Token Agents
  • Mint new tokens
  • Burn tokens
  • Force transfer tokens
  • Pause/unpause transfers
Compliance Agents
  • Update compliance rules
  • Add/remove claim topics
  • Manage trusted issuers
  • Configure transfer restrictions
